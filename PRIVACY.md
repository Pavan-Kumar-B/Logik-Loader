# Privacy Policy for Logik Loader

**Last Updated:** November 23, 2025

Logik Loader ("we", "our", or "the extension") is a developer utility designed to streamline configuration management within the Logik.io platform. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains how our extension handles your data.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or business data.**

- **No Personal Information:** The extension does not collect your name, email address, IP address, or physical location.
- **No Configuration Data:** All JSON payloads, session drafts, and configuration data processed by the extension remain strictly within your local browser environment. We do not transmit this data to any external servers, cloud databases, or third-party analytics services.
- **No Tracking:** We do not use cookies, pixels, or tracking scripts to monitor your browsing activity.

## 2. Permissions and Technical Operation

To function correctly, the extension requires specific browser permissions. Here is how they are used:

- **`scripting` & `activeTab`:** These permissions are used solely to embed the necessary utility scripts into the specific Logik.io configuration page you are currently interacting with. This allows the tool to apply your JSON payloads to the application directly within your browser tab.
- **`storage`:** This permission is used locally to save your interface preferences (specifically, the state of the "Debug Mode" toggle). This preference data is stored on your device and is never sent to us.
- **Host Permissions (`https://*.logik.io/*`):** The extension is restricted to operate only on Logik.io domains. It does not access, read, or modify content on any other websites you visit.

## 3. Data Security

All processing of configuration data happens client-side (within your computer's memory). Since we do not collect or store your data on our servers, there is no risk of your proprietary configuration data being breached from our side.

## 4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our technical operations or legal obligations. Any changes will be posted on this page.
